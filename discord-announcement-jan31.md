# 🔴 DLHD IS BACK ONLINE

The cat-and-mouse game continues, and we just caught the cheese again. 🧀

## What Happened

Around January 28th, DLHD pushed a significant update to their stream protection system. All DLHD streams on Flyx went down while we worked on a fix.

After some late nights and a lot of reverse engineering, we've cracked their new system and streams are back up and running.

We're getting faster at adapting, and their patches are taking longer to kick us out. The trend is in our favor. 📈
